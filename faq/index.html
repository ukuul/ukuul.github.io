Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ukuul?

A simple mobile app for sending dispatch riders on delivery errands.

Which parts of Accra does Ukuul cover?

Most of Accra, including Tema and Kasoa, is under Ukuul’s coverage.

When is Ukuul available?

Monday - Saturday, 9am - 5pm

What can a motorcycle carry?

Anything within 17 inches width, 15 inches length and 15 inches height. Up to 40kg in weight. 80% of packages can be delivered with Ukuul. No living things. No contraband.

Does Ukuul fulfill Cash on Delivery?

Yes. We charge 2% for handling and processing.

Can Ukuul pay cash for me?

No. Kindly, use mobile money or another money transfer method.
